Privacy Ferret has been installed successfully! (BETA)
Privacy Ferret is a Pale Moon–focused fork of Privacy Badger by the EFF.
  • Protects your privacy online by learning to block hidden third-party trackers.
  • No user config required — learns as you browse.
  • Anti-tracking for Pale Moon users.
  • Forked for Pale Moon’s XUL-based extension ecosystem.
